Vendor Brief: AcmeDoc AI

Summary: AcmeDoc AI provides document automation workflows for contract intake, extraction, and routing with an LLM-based rules engine.

Capabilities

  • Contract ingestion: PDF, DOCX, email attachment
  • Extraction: key terms (parties, effective date, term, renewal, termination, governing law)
  • Human-in-the-loop review: configurable queues and approvals
  • Integrations: REST API and webhook callbacks; prebuilt connectors for common CRMs

Reliability and SLAs

  • Target uptime: 99.9% monthly uptime
  • Support: 24x5 standard; 24x7 available as add-on

Security and Compliance

  • Stated compliance: SOC 2 Type II (last audit date: 2025-10-15)
  • Data residency: US and EU regions supported
  • Encryption: in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)

Pricing (indicative)

  • Base platform fee: $36,000 per year
  • Usage: $0.08 per document processed
  • Includes: 2,000 documents/month, standard support

Risks / Open Items

  • OCR for scanned documents is offered via an optional add-on and may increase cost
  • Some customers report that complex tables require reviewer confirmation
