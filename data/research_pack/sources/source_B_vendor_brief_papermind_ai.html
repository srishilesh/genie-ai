Vendor Brief: PaperMind AI

Summary: PaperMind AI focuses on contract understanding with strong clause detection and a configurable review console.

Capabilities

  • Contract ingestion: PDF and DOCX
  • Clause library: prebuilt clause patterns for renewals and termination
  • Extraction: key terms plus clause-level “risk flags”
  • Integrations: REST API; webhook callbacks

Reliability and SLAs

  • Target uptime: 99.5% monthly uptime
  • Support: 8x5 standard; 24x5 available as add-on

Security and Compliance

  • Stated compliance: SOC 2 Type I (in progress for Type II)
  • Data residency: US only
  • Encryption: in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest

Pricing (indicative)

  • Base platform fee: $24,000 per year
  • Usage: $0.12 per document processed
  • Includes: 1,000 documents/month, standard support

Risks / Open Items

  • Limited EU data residency
  • Some integrations may require professional services
